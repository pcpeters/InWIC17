MORE ABOUT LIGHTNING TALKS

Lightning Talks are five minute mini-presentations that can range from a class project, research, inside information on how to survive a computing major, programming tips, ideas on how to create a successful women in computing group, etc.

  • Advice on How to Give a Good Lightning Talk by Mark Fowler
  • How to Conquer Public Speaking Fear (SlideShare) by Morton C. Orman, M.D.

You are encouraged to use one of the two templates below as you prepare your lightning talk. Each template has the transition timing already set up wherein slides automatically change every 15 or 30 seconds. You may modify the slide backgrounds and content as appropriate for your talk and should change the footers to identify your name, school, and title, but do NOT change the timing of the transitions or add/remove slides.

  • 20 slides that change every 15 seconds
